### 3. **Beale Function (3 variables)**
Beale's function is another commonly used benchmark for optimization.

\[
f(x_1, x_2, x_3) = (1.5 - x_1 + x_1 x_2)^2 + (2.25 - x_1 + x_1 x_2^2)^2 + (2.625 - x_1 + x_1 x_2^3)^2 + x_3^2
\]

- **Variables**: \( x_1, x_2, x_3 \)
- **Global minimum**: \( f(3, 0.5, 0) = 0 \)
